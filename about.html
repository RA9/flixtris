Our Story

From a simple idea to a global gaming phenomenon

Flixtris was born from a love for the classic Tetris game that has captivated millions for decades. Our founder, a passionate developer and puzzle enthusiast, realized that while the core gameplay was timeless, the presentation and multiplayer experience could be revolutionized for the modern web.

What started as a weekend project in 2023 quickly evolved into a full-featured gaming platform. We combined the addictive mechanics of Tetris with cutting-edge web technologies, real-time multiplayer capabilities, and innovative game modes that keep players coming back for more.

Today, Flixtris stands as a testament to how classic games can be reimagined for the digital age, offering both nostalgic comfort and fresh excitement to players worldwide.

Our Mission

To create the most accessible, engaging, and innovative block puzzle game that brings people together through the universal language of gaming. We believe that great games should be free to play, work on any device, and provide endless hours of fun for players of all skill levels.

We're committed to continuous improvement, listening to our community, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in browser-based gaming. Join us in stacking blocks and building memories!
